Sports Week

Sports Week is one of the most exciting events in our college, bringing students together through games, teamwork, and healthy competition. Throughout the week, various indoor and outdoor sports such as football, basketball, volleyball, and athletics are organized, allowing students to showcase their talents and sportsmanship. Sports Week not only promotes physical fitness but also helps students develop discipline, leadership, and unity. The cheerful atmosphere, enthusiastic participation, and supportive crowd make Sports Week a memorable experience for everyone in the college.
